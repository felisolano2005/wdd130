Rapid Rush Adventures

At Rapid Rush Adventures, our mission is to be the highlight of your Costa Rica vacation, offering unforgettable rafting and kayaking adventures with top-notch safety standards. We exclusively work with Certified Professional Guides who are experienced, friendly, and ready to ensure your safety and enjoyment.

History

Founded in 2012, Rapid Rush Adventures began with a simple idea: share the thrill of the river while respecting the power of nature. What started as two friends with one raft and a passion for whitewater quickly grew into one of the regions most trusted adventure outfitters.

Today, the company remains rooted in its original mission: delivering thrilling adventures while protecting the natural beauty of Costa Ricas rivers. Though it has grown significantly since its humble beginnings, Rapid Rush Adventures still carries the same spirit of friendship, courage, and respect for the river that started it all.
